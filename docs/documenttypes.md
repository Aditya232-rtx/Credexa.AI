## **1. Land & Property Records** 

Land records are highly regional, unstructured or semi-structured documents detailing ownership, encumbrances, and geographical parameters. They are the primary targets for high-value real estate and collateral fraud. 

## **Government Land Registries & Ownership Certificates** 

- **7/12 Extract (Satbara Utara):** (Regional: India/Maharashtra/Gujarat) Combined document containing Form VII (owner details, rights, liabilities) and Form XII (agricultural details, crop types, loan encumbrances). 

- **8A Extract:** (Regional: India) A consolidated statement showing the total land holding of an individual across various survey numbers within a village. 

- **Property Card (Malmatta Patrak):** Urban land ownership records detailing structural boundaries, municipal holdings, and historical title transfers. 

- **Record of Rights (RoR) / Jamabandi:** The foundational registry across northern and eastern regions detailing land cultivation rights, shares, and revenue liabilities. 

- **Patta / Chitta:** (Regional: Southern India) Government-issued land title records establishing legal ownership and land classification (Nanjai/wetland or Punjai/dryland). 

- **Khata Certificate & Extract:** (Regional: Urban India) Municipal documents certifying an individual's property assessment details for tax obligations. 

## **Conveyance & Legal Property Deeds** 

- **Sale Deed (Deed of Conveyance):** The core legal instrument executing the absolute transfer of property ownership from a seller to a buyer. 

- **Gift Deed / Relinquishment Deed:** Legal instruments executing the transfer of property without financial consideration or the formal surrendering of ancestral rights. 

- **Lease Deed / Rental Agreement:** Long-term or short-term commercial/residential tenancy contracts establishing possessory rights. 

- **Mortgage Deed:** The legal binding agreement establishing the bank's security interest or lien over the property as collateral for capital deployment. 

## **Geographical & Survey Clearances** 

- **Cadastral Maps / Tipan / FMB (Field Measurement Book):** Scaled, 

   - government-engineered graphical maps detailing the exact geometric boundaries and dimensions of land parcels. 

- **Non-Agricultural (NA) Permission:** Official regulatory clearance converting land utility status from agricultural to commercial or residential. 

- **Encumbrance Certificate (EC):** A historical ledger report (typically spanning 15–30 years) certifying that the property is completely free from unsatisfied monetary liabilities or legal disputes. 

## **2. Legal & Identity Documents** 

Legal documents establish the baseline truth regarding identity, corporate status, statutory authorization, and civil relationships. They lack mathematical controls and must be forensic-photograph verified.

## **Statutory Civil Identity & Citizenship Certificates** 

- **National Identity Smart Cards:** Standardized regulatory biometric identity profiles (e.g., Aadhaar Card, PAN Card, Social Security Cards, Resident Identity Profiles). 

- **International Travel Passports:** Cryptographically secured or standard machine-readable zone (MRZ) documents verifying cross-border citizenship. 

- **Voter Registration IDs / Driving Licenses:** Regional government-issued physical credentials establishing residency, identity, and localized authorizations. 

- **Civil Status Certificates:** Foundational vital statistics documentation (Birth Certificates, Marriage Certificates, Death Certificates). 

## **Corporate Status & Entity Governance Documents** 

- **Memorandum of Association (MoA) & Articles of Association (AoA):** The foundational corporate charter defining the exact operational scope and internal governing regulations of an enterprise. 

- **Certificate of Incorporation (CoI):** The legal birth certificate of a corporate entity issued by national regulatory authorities (e.g., Ministry of Corporate Affairs/MCA, SEC). 

- **Partnership Deeds / LLP Agreements:** Contractual frameworks executing the operational rights, capital ratios, and dissolution parameters of non-incorporated structures. 

- **Board Resolutions:** Formally certified internal corporate mandates authorizing specific executives to execute financial borrowings, open accounts, or pledge assets. 

- **Shareholder Agreements & Cap Tables:** Detailed registers mapping equity ownership percentages, vesting tracks, and internal corporate control mechanisms. 

## **Judicial, Contractual, & Authorization Instruments** 

- **Power of Attorney (PoA):** Legal instruments delegating absolute or specific decision-making authority from a principal to an agent or attorney-in-fact. 

- **Affidavits & Declarations:** Sworn, legally binding statements executed before authorized judicial officers or notaries public. 

- **Indemnity Bonds:** Contractual agreements obligating one party to financially compensate or protect another against future targeted liabilities or operational losses.
